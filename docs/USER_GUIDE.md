# ImmersiAds — User Guide

Learn a new language through authentic video advertisements.

---

## Installation

1. Download the latest APK from the [Releases](https://github.com/anomalyco/immersi-ads-android/releases) page.
2. On your Android device, enable **Install from unknown sources** (Settings → Security → Unknown sources).
3. Open the APK file and tap **Install**.
4. Open the **ImmersiAds** app from your app drawer.

> **Minimum requirements:** Android 7.0 (API 24) or newer.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Onboarding

When you open the app for the first time, you will be guided through a quick setup:

1. **Select your native language** — The language you already speak fluently.
2. **Select your target language** — The language you want to learn (Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, Russian, or Hindi).

After completing onboarding, you will be taken to the advertisement feed.

### 2. Advertisement Feed

The feed shows video advertisements in your target language. You can:

- **Filter** by language using the filter button (tap to toggle the filter on/off).
- **Browse** the list of available ads — each card shows the title, difficulty level, and language.
- **Tap** any ad card to open the video player.

### 3. Video Player

The player is the core learning experience:

- **Play/Pause** — Tap the video to toggle playback.
- **Speed control** — Use the speed button to cycle through 0.5×, 0.75×, 1.0×, 1.25×, 1.5×, and 2.0× speed.
- **Replay** — Tap the replay button to restart the current ad.
- **Subtitles** — Toggle subtitles on/off with the subtitles button. When enabled, you will see the dialogue in both your target language and native language.
- **Tap a word** in the subtitle to see its translation in a popup.
- **Save vocabulary** — From the translation popup, tap the save button to add the word to your personal vocabulary list.

### 4. Vocabulary

Your saved words and phrases are available in the Vocabulary tab:

- **Browse** all saved vocabulary items.
- **Search** by word or translation using the search bar.
- **Delete** individual items by tapping the delete icon.
- Each item shows the word in your target language, its translation, the source ad, and the date added.

### 5. Progress Dashboard

Track your learning journey:

- **Current streak** — Consecutive days of activity.
- **Ads watched** — Total number of advertisements viewed.
- **Words saved** — Total vocabulary items collected.
- **Milestones** — Special achievements as you progress.

### 6. Settings

Customize your experience in the Settings screen:

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Native language** | Change your base language |
| **Target language** | Change the language you are learning |
| **Default playback speed** | Set the starting speed for new videos |
| **Subtitles on by default** | Choose whether subtitles are shown automatically |
| **Dark mode** | Switch between light, dark, or system theme |

## Tips for Effective Learning

- **Start at 0.75× speed** — Slower playback helps you catch individual words.
- **Replay ads multiple times** — Each repetition reinforces vocabulary.
- **Tap unfamiliar words** — Build your vocabulary list actively.
- **Review your vocabulary regularly** — Saved words are available anytime without watching the ad again.
- **Set a daily goal** — Even one ad per day builds your streak and keeps you consistent.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| App crashes on startup | Ensure your device runs Android 7.0+ and has at least 2 GB of RAM |
| Video won't play | Check that the video file is not corrupted; try reinstalling the app |
| Subtitles don't appear | Make sure subtitles are enabled in Settings or via the player button |
| Saved words are missing | Vocabulary is stored locally; uninstalling the app erases your data |
| Can't find my language | ImmersiAds currently supports English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, Russian, and Hindi |

## FAQ

**Does the app require an internet connection?**
No. The app ships with sample advertisements so you can explore all features offline.

**Will there be more advertisements?**
Future versions will integrate with real advertisement APIs for fresh content.

**Is my vocabulary data backed up?**
Not yet. Vocabulary is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is planned for a future release.

**How do I reset the app?**
Go to Settings → tap **Reset onboarding** to restart from the beginning. To fully reset, clear the app data in your device Settings → Apps → ImmersiAds → Storage → Clear data.
